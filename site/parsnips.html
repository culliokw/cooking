parsnips

Parsnips are a close relative to carrots, and parsley. They can be cooked, or eaten raw. Its flavor is sweet, especially when cooked. They can be baked, boiled, pureed, roasted, fried, grilled, or steamed. Parnips are high in potassium.

Before the arrival of cane sugar in Europe, parsnips were used as a sweetener!
